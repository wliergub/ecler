Transient Forms

September 1-30, 2024

About the Exhibition

A contemporary exploration of form and light through the human figure. This collection challenges traditional notions of beauty and temporality, capturing the ephemeral nature of human expression in controlled studio environments.

Curated by renowned photographer Alessandra Violetta, the exhibition features works from emerging artists across 12 countries.
